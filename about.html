This game has been carefully designed with the fundamental purpose of generating a deeper understanding of the challenges faced by those struggling with mental health issues. Through a combination of narrative and metaphorical games, it seeks to educate, raise awareness, and provide practical strategies to confront these challenges.

Each challenge in the game symbolizes a common internal struggle experienced by individuals facing mental health problems. From representing anxiety as an obstacle to be overcome to depicting mazes symbolizing mental confusion, every element of the game is designed to reflect and symbolize the emotional complexities involved in this battle.

The purpose of "Mente en Equilibrio" is not only to provide entertainment but also to create a safe space where players can explore and better understand the realities of mental health. By playing this game, participants will have the opportunity to gain a more empathetic and understanding perspective towards those grappling with these challenges in their daily lives.
